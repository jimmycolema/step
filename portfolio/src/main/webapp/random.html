Home Backpacking Humans I Love

People are the Anchor.

If I've learned anything in my twenty years of existing, it's that the places you'll find yourself are only ever temporary. Homes, belongings, and material possessions have come and gone, sometimes in the matter of seconds, throughout my life. My friends and my family are my dearest possessions, my reason for getting up in the morning, my anchors. Life is nuts, but it's those you love along the way that make it worth it.
