Jimmy Coleman

How strange it is to be anything at all!

Hey all!

My name is Jimmy Coleman, and I'm a Google STEP intern (Summer 2020)! When I'm not coding or doing proofs, I can be found in the mountains backpacking, playing on my guitar, or brewing a fresh cup of Maxwell House coffee!

Growing up, I lived in a trailer with five other people and a dog in the small town of Middletown, Delaware. There I learned not to need much, and that life's most precious blessings are in the people we get close to along the way! After moving to Maryland to live with my Dad halfway through the ninth grade, I was shown a powerful lesson regarding life's tendency to be ever-changing. These experiences have come to make me feel very comfortable wandering and roaming anywhere I can get to. I'm always looking for new faces to meet, new trails to tread, and new stories to tell!

While exploring some of my favorite exploits, I'll leave you with an old backpacker proverb that I've held onto for some time now: if you can't carry it in your heart or in your pack, you don't need it.
